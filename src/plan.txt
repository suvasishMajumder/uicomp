Here are some **highly reusable components** you can add to your UI library to showcase your frontend skills:

---

### 1. **Alert / Toast**
- For showing notifications, errors, or success messages.

### 2. **Modal / Dialog**
- For pop-up dialogs, confirmations, or forms.

### 3. **Checkbox & Radio**
- Custom styled checkbox and radio button components.

### 4. **Select / Dropdown**
- For selecting options from a list.

### 5. **Avatar**
- For user profile pictures, with fallback initials.

### 6. **Badge**
- For status indicators, counts, or labels.

### 7. **Tabs**
- For tabbed navigation/content switching.

### 8. **Tooltip**
- For showing extra info on hover/focus.

### 9. **Loader / Spinner**
- For indicating loading states.

### 10. **Navbar / AppBar**
- For top navigation bars.

### 11. **Breadcrumbs**
- For showing navigation paths.

### 12. **Pagination**
- For navigating through pages of data.

### 13. **Accordion**
- For collapsible content sections.

### 14. **Switch / Toggle**
- For on/off switches.

### 15. **Slider / Range Input**
- For selecting a value or range.

---

**Tip:**  
Make each component:
- Accept flexible props (`children`, `className`, `style`, etc.)
- Be accessible (keyboard, ARIA)
- Support theming/variants

Adding even a few of these will make your UI library look much more complete and professional!
